How to claim your lootboxes in game!

First Login into the game, if you don't have an account, create one.

Go into the export/import tab

Select the Import option in the slider and the correct network (IOTA mainnet)

Then you will see your lootboxes in the grid, select them and click import

Now your lootboxes will be avaible in your inventory!, just go to the misc section and click them to open. Enjoy!
