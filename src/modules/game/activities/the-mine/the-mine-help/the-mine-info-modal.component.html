How to stake your Golden Uruks!

Step 1: Go to the "Bridge" tab to export your Golden Uruks to your wallet.

Step 2: Ensure you are connected to a valid network (e.g., IOTA) by selecting it from the network options.

Step 3: Once your Uruks are in your wallet and you're connected to a network, go to the mine section and enter the amount you want to stake.

Step 4: Enjoy the rewards! Your Golden Uruks are now staked, and you will start earning materials and treasures.
