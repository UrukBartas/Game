Getting Started: How to Stake

1
Bridge Your Uruks

Go to the "Bridge" tab to export your Golden Uruks to your wallet.

2
Connect to Network

Ensure you are connected to a valid network (e.g., IOTA) by selecting it from the network options.

3
Stake Your Uruks

Once your Uruks are in your wallet and you're connected, enter the amount you want to stake.
