Welcome to The Mine!

The Mine is our unique staking system where you invest your tokens to earn exclusive rewards. By staking, you increase your workforce and unlock higher tiers that provide better benefits such as more materials per hour, higher treasure chances, enhanced experience and Golden Uruks boosts, and increased export limits.

  1. Staking Tokens:

    In The Mine, you stake your tokens to hire goblin workers and build your mining infrastructure. Your staked amount determines your tier and the rewards you receive.

  2. Reward Benefits:

    Each tier offers multiple benefits:

    • Materials per Hour: Increase your resource extraction with a higher matFactor.
    • Treasure Chance: A greater lootbox chance improves your odds of finding rare items.
    • Experience Boost: Earn extra experience to level up faster.
    • Golden Uruks Boost: Enjoy higher Uruks gain per hour.
    • Travel Time Reduction: Shorter travel times help you collect rewards more efficiently.
    • Export Limits: Higher tiers allow you to export more Golden Uruks each day.
