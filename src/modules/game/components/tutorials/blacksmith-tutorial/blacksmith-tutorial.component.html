Welcome to the Blacksmith!

In the Blacksmith, you can work on your items in various ways to improve or recycle them. Here are the main functions available:

  1. Melt:

    Break down unwanted items into magical dust, which can then be used as a resource for enhancements or upgrades. Use this option when you want to recycle items that no longer serve you.

  2. Repair:

    Restore the durability of your items so they can perform optimally in battle. Repairing is crucial when your gear has been worn down by repeated combat.

  3. Combine:

    Fuse two or more items together in hopes of creating a more powerful or rare item. This option requires selecting at least two items to combine.

  4. Upgrade:

    Enhance an item's stats or quality by investing valuable resources. However, be cautious—if the item's durability is too low, it may break during the upgrade process.

  5. Enchant:

    Apply unique enchantments to your items to boost their performance, attributes, or abilities. Enchanting adds special effects that can give you an edge in battle.

Note: Upgrading items carries a risk—if an item’s durability is insufficient, it may break during the process. Always check your item's condition before attempting an upgrade.
