Welcome to The Crypt!

The Crypt is a challenging, rogue-like PvE adventure where you face instant encounters and battle increasingly tougher enemies. As you delve deeper, you'll fight for survival and glory—earning powerful upgrades, Golden Uruks, and rare rewards!

  1. Entering the Crypt:

    Once you step into the Crypt, there's no turning back. Your current state is saved, and you cannot trade or equip new items. You must rely solely on your existing gear to survive the horrors below.

  2. Instant Encounters:

    In this rogue-like experience, encounters occur instantly as you progress from one room to the next. Be prepared for sudden battles where every moment counts.

  3. Reward Selection:

    After each battle, you'll have the opportunity to choose a reward that will enhance your abilities for the next fight. These upgrades can boost your stats, provide new abilities, or offer other powerful enhancements.

  4. Progressive Difficulty & Weekly Rewards:

    Each subsequent battle grows tougher than the previous one. The enemies become stronger, and strategic combat is essential. Moreover, the Crypt resets every week, and rewards are distributed based on how deep you manage to descend.

Equip your best gear and stock up on consumables—prepare to face the Crypt’s relentless challenges. Prove your worth, conquer your fears, and reap the rewards that await in the depths!
