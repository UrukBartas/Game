Welcome to the Shop!

In the Shop, you can purchase various in-game items such as gear, materials, potions, and other miscellaneous items using Golden Uruks. Browse through the different tabs to find what you need and add items to your cart for purchase.

  1. Shop Interface Overview:

    The Shop features a dynamic background along with a friendly dialog that greets you when you enter. When you select items, a shop cart appears showing the names of the items and the total price.

  2. Item Tabs:

    Use the tabs at the top of the Shop to switch between different categories:

    • Gear: View and purchase equippable items.
    • Mats: Browse and buy crafting materials.
    • Potions: Find consumables like potions and boosters.
    • Miscellany: Discover recipes, boosts, and other assorted items.
  3. Daily and Premium Rolls:

    The Shop includes special roll features to refresh your available items:

    • Daily Roll: A free roll that randomly refreshes the Shop items, often accompanied by a friendly greeting.
    • Premium Roll: This roll costs Golden Uruks and improves your chances of obtaining rare items. The current roll price and available number of rolls are displayed.
  4. Purchasing Items:

    When you select items, they are added to your Shop cart, which shows the item names and total price (in Golden Uruks). Click the purchase button to confirm your order. A confirmation dialog will appear to ensure you want to proceed with the purchase.

Explore the Shop to enhance your character with new gear, stock up on materials for crafting, and take advantage of daily and premium roll offers. Happy shopping!
