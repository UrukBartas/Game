Welcome to the Bridge!

In the Bridge section, you can seamlessly export your in-game rewards to your external wallet. Golden Uruks earned during gameplay are automatically converted into ERC20 tokens and sent to your wallet, while your items are minted and exported as NFTs.

  1. Exporting Golden Uruks:

    When you select the "Export" mode, your Golden Uruks are converted into ERC20 tokens. These tokens are transferred directly to your connected wallet, allowing you to manage, trade, or use them outside the game.

  2. Exporting Items as NFTs:

    Choose the NFT export type to automatically mint your valuable in-game items as NFTs. This process creates a unique, on-chain record of your items, ensuring secure ownership and easy tradability.

  3. Import Mode:

    You can toggle between Export and Import modes using the switch at the top. In Import mode, items or tokens are brought back into your game inventory automatically.

Manage your rewards efficiently by exporting your Golden Uruks as tokens and your items as NFTs with just a few clicks!
