Welcome to the Quest System!

This is a PvE section where you face all kinds of creatures to earn Golden Uruks, equippable items, materials, and more. The Quest System is your gateway to epic adventures. Depending on your progress, you’ll experience several phases: selecting a quest, tracking its progress, engaging in combat, and reviewing the results.

  1. Quest Picker:

    In this phase, you choose your next quest from a list of available adventures. Each quest card displays key details such as the quest name, level requirement, description, and an estimated completion time.

  2. Quest Progress:

    After selecting a quest, the progress view kicks in. A carousel displays the chosen quest’s background image, title, and detailed description. The screen features a progress bar that shows the remaining time, as well as any active boosts that reduce quest duration.

  3. Quest Fight:

    For quests that involve combat, you transition into the fight phase. Here, you face your enemy—complete with details about its type and hints about its strengths and weaknesses. Engage in battle using your equipped items and abilities.

  4. Quest Result:

    Upon completing the quest or battle, the result screen is displayed. Whether you’ve achieved victory or suffered defeat, you’ll see valuable information such as loot dropped, experience gained, Uruks earned, and level-up notifications. In some cases, you might even encounter a special “ganked” scenario.

Embark on your quests, conquer challenges, and reap the rewards of your adventures. Good luck, brave adventurer!
