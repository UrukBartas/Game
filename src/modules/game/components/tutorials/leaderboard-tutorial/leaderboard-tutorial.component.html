Welcome to the Hall of Uruks!

In the Hall of Uruks, you can see how you rank against other players in both PvE and PvP modes. Use the tabs to switch between rankings, apply time filters, and search by username or wallet address.

  1. Leaderboard Modes:

    Choose between PvE and PvP leaderboards. The PvE tab displays rankings based on player levels and completed quests, while the PvP tab ranks players by their arena points and MMR.

  2. Time Filters:

    Use the time filter tabs (Today, 30d, 7d) to view rankings over different periods.

  3. Search Functionality:

    Enter a username or wallet address in the search box to quickly locate specific players.

  4. Player Ranking and Details:

    Each leaderboard entry shows the player's rank, profile image, and online status (indicated by a colored dot). Top players on the first page are awarded medals (gold, silver, bronze), and your own entry is highlighted. You can view a player's profile by clicking the eye icon or challenge them in PvP mode.

  5. Pagination:

    Use the left and right arrows at the bottom to navigate through multiple pages of rankings.

Check your ranking, challenge other players, and strive to climb the Hall of Uruks. Whether you're excelling in PvE or dominating in PvP, here is where legends are made!
