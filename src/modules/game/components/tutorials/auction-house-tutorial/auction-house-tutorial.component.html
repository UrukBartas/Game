Welcome to the Auction House!

The Auction House is your in-game marketplace for trading items. Here, you can create new trades, view detailed listing information, filter and sort listings, and check bids. Let’s walk through the main functions:

  1. Creating a New Trade:

    Click the "New Trade" button to open a modal where you can list your item for sale. This is your starting point for selling items in the marketplace.

  2. Viewing Listings:

    Browse the market listings and click on any listing to view more details. This opens a modal displaying the item’s information, seller details, and current bid status.

  3. Filtering and Searching:

    Use the search box to find listings by name and apply filters such as minimum/maximum level, rarity, and status (e.g., Active, Sold, With bids, Bought). The left sidebar also offers preset filters for various item categories and subtypes.

  4. Sorting Listings:

    Toggle between sorting by recent or price using the sort icon and label at the top of the listings. You can also change the sort order (ascending/descending) with a simple click.

  5. Pagination:

    Navigate through the pages of listings using the pagination controls at the bottom. This helps you quickly browse through a large number of items available in the market.

Explore the Auction House to buy, sell, or bid on items, and take full advantage of the marketplace to enhance your gameplay!
