Normal PVP:

Normal PVP is a real-time battle against an opponent. When you challenge someone, they will receive a notification with the option to accept or decline the challenge.

Autobattle:

Autobattle gives you the opportunity to fight an enemy each hour. This opponent will have more arena points than you, and the battle will be automatic, with no player input required.
